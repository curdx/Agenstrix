Output: A backend that cleans up cleanly on Ctrl+C even when the PTY child has its own children, plus a `bunx agenstrix doctor --reap` command that finds and offers to kill orphans.

<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `~/.agenstrix/running.json` | Tracks PIDs that the user's backend spawned; doctor reaper reads this and may kill processes the user owns. No privilege escalation possible (process.kill respects UID). |
| Stale `.git/index.lock` | Removing it without checking the lock-holder PID could break a concurrent `git` operation. Phase 1: only warn + provide manual `rm` command; Phase 2 will inspect the lock's contents to confirm the holder is dead. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
